About TLC Online

TLC is an online portal for both mental health patients and mental health specialists.

We provide resources, information, and support to people who are struggling with mental health issues. We offer a wide range of services, including online counseling, peer support forums, self-help tools, educational resources, and access to mental health professionals.

TLC a shorthand way of referring to “Tender Loving Care” used particularly in informal or personal contexts. It is a phrase is often used to describe the compassionate and attentive care given to someone or something that is in need of special attention, such as a sick or injured person, a child, a pet, or a valuable possession – in this instance, MENTAL HEALTH.
